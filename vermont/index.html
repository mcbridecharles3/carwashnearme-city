Top Tips for Keeping Your Vermont Vehicle Clean and Protected

  • Wash frequently: Winter salt and grime can damage your paint if left too long — aim for weekly washes in the cold months.
  • Invest in regular detailing: Quarterly interior and exterior details help maintain your car’s value and appearance.
  • Protect your paint: Use wax or ceramic coatings to shield against harsh weather and UV rays.
  • Don’t forget the interior: Vacuum, shampoo carpets, and condition leather to keep your car fresh and comfortable.
  • Choose eco-friendly washes: Many Vermont car washes use biodegradable soaps and water-saving techniques to protect our beautiful environment.
  • Consider mobile detailing: Save time by booking pros who come directly to your home or office.
