Tips for Keeping Your Car Clean Year-Round in Ohio

  • Wash regularly: Don’t wait for grime to build up — clean weekly during winter and pollen-heavy seasons.
  • Detail quarterly: Schedule full details every 3 months for long-term protection and resale value.
  • Protect your paint: Use wax or ceramic coatings to keep your finish glossy and guard against road debris and UV rays.
  • Keep interiors fresh: Vacuum carpets, wipe down touchscreens, and condition leather seats often.
  • Try a waterless wash: Great for winter when hoses freeze or during water restrictions.
