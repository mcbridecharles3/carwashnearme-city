💡 Want to Preserve the Wax on Your Car?

If you’ve taken the time to wax your car, the last thing you want is to strip it off at the car wash.

Choose a hand car wash. Hand car washes typically use gentle, wax-safe soaps that are pH balanced and designed to clean without stripping protective wax layers.

Avoid automatic car washes if preserving wax is a priority. Most automatic systems use acidic or harsh alkaline detergents that aggressively clean — and remove wax in the process.

For long-lasting shine and protection, stick with a professional hand wash or do it yourself using wax-friendly products.
