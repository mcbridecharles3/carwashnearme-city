💡 Pro Tips for Washing Your Car in Detroit

  • Go early or late: Midday sun can leave soap spots. Wash in the morning or evening for best results.
  • Use touchless if in a rush: Need speed and no scratches? Touchless washes are fast and safe for newer paint jobs.
  • Always dry after washing: Use a microfiber towel or let the pros handle it to prevent water spots and streaks.
  • Winter tip: Get an undercarriage rinse to protect against road salt and rust!

🧼 How to Pick the Right Auto Detailer

Not all detailers are created equal. Here’s how to find your perfect match:

  • Ask what’s included: Does the package cover carpets, mats, vents, or just the basics?
  • Check reviews and photos: Look for local results with before-and-after pictures.
  • Find specialists: Want ceramic coating, paint correction, or engine bay cleaning? Ask if they specialize.
  • Ask how long it takes: Full details can take 2–6 hours. A good detailer never rushes perfection.
  • Bonus: If they use steam or ozone treatments, that’s a win for deep-cleaning and allergens.

🌟 First Time at a Car Wash or Detailer?

No sweat! Here’s what to expect:

  • For car washes: Pull in slowly, follow the signs, and keep your windows rolled up. Yes, people forget that.
  • For detailers: Remove your valuables, ask for a walk-through, and make sure they explain the process.
  • Payment: Many offer memberships or discounts if you return regularly. Ask about deals.
  • Before/after check: Take photos for peace of mind, especially if it’s your first time.
