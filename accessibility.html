Our Commitment to Accessibility

At CarWashNearMe.City, we want everyone to be able to search, browse, and find local car wash and detailing services—regardless of ability.

What We're Doing

We’re actively working to make our site more accessible by following best practices based on the WCAG 2.1 standards. This includes:

  • Using clear navigation and headings
  • Providing alt text for images
  • Ensuring good color contrast and readable fonts
  • Testing compatibility with screen readers and mobile devices

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. If you spot something we missed or need help accessing any part of our site, let us know—we’re here to help.
